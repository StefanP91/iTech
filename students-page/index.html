STUDENT RESOURCES

We offer a variety of Student Resources to help you succeed both academically and personally:

Tutoring Services:

Available after school for subjects like math, science, and writing. Check the tutoring schedule posted in the main office or on the student portal.

Online Learning Platforms:

Use platforms like Khan Academy, Coursera, and Edmodo for extra practice and enrichment.

Library Resources:

Access digital books, journals, and research materials from the school library. Visit the Library Portal to browse resources.

Study Guides & Practice Tests:

Use our Study Guides to prepare for your AP exams, SATs, and certifications.

MENTAL HEALTH AND WELLNESS

Your well-being is important! Here are resources available to support you:

Counseling Services:

Reach out to Ms. Tyra Tate, our school counselor, for emotional and academic support.

Self-Care Tips:

Check out articles and resources on stress management, time management, and emotional health.

Student Assistance Programs (SAP):

Our confidential programs provide support for students facing personal challenges. Contact our counselor for more details.
