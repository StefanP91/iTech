WHAT ARE MAGNET PROGRAMS?

Magnet programs are specialized academic tracks that focus on students' talents, interests, and career aspirations. These programs provide advanced, challenging coursework and offer students the chance to gain real-world experience in specialized fields, preparing them for both higher education and successful careers in the tech industry. The best part? All of this is provided at no cost as part of the Miami-Dade County Public Schools system.

WHO CAN ATTEND MAGNET PROGRAMS?

Magnet programs are open to eligible Miami-Dade County Public Schools (MDCPS) students in grades 9-12. Whether you're a current middle school student transitioning to high school or a high school student looking for a specialized tech program, iTech Academy may be the perfect fit for you!

HOW TO APPLY TO ITECH ACADEMY

Application Period (Regular Cycle)

  • October 1 – January 15: The regular application cycle for all Magnet programs is open.
  • You can apply online via the MDCPS Parent Portal or submit a paper application to the School Choice and Parental Options Office.

Off-Cycle Application (April 2024)

  • If you missed the regular application window, Off-Cycle applications for the 2024-2025 school year will open in April 2024. Apply Now (Off-Cycle)

DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

In addition to the application, private school students or students entering MDCPS for the first time need to provide their final 7th and 8th grade report cards. You can contact our office at 305-762-5000 for more information on required documents.

HOW ARE STUDENTS SELECTED FOR ITECH ACADEMY?

After ensuring eligibility, we use a random selection process for student placements. Here's how the process works:

Eligibility Review:

All applications are reviewed for eligibility based on
academic performance and other requirements.

Random Selection:

If there are more applicants than available spots,
students are selected randomly.

Notification:

Students will be notified of their acceptance, waitlist
status, or decline by mid-March.
