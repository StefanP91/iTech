WELCOME TO THE PARENT’S CORNER

This section is designed to keep you informed and engaged in your child's educational journey at iTech @ Thomas A. Edison Educational Center. Here, you'll find helpful resources, important school information, and opportunities to support your child's academic and extracurricular success.

IMPORTANT SCHOOL INFORMATION

School Policies and Guidelines

Student Code
of Conduct

Familiarize yourself with the Student Code of Conduct to understand the behavior and discipline expectations for students.

Parent-Student
Handbook:

Access the comprehensive Parent-Student Handbook for details about school rules, policies, safety guidelines, and school procedures. This document helps you understand what’s expected from students and parents.

Uniform and
Dress Code:

Learn about the required Uniform and Dress Code for iTech students. Ensure that your child is prepared for a professional learning environment.

Anti-Discrimination

iTech is committed to creating an inclusive and respectful environment for all. Review our Anti-Discrimination Policy in English, Spanish, or Creole.

PARENT INVOLVEMENT

We encourage parents to be active participants in their child's education.
There are several ways you can engage with iTech:

PTSA (PARENT TEACHER
STUDENT ASSOCIATION):

Join our PTSA to collaborate with other parents, teachers, and staff in organizing events, fundraising, and creating a positive school community.

VOLUNTEER
OPPORTUNITIES:

Parents are encouraged to volunteer for events, help with school activities, or provide mentorship opportunities for students. Your involvement makes a big difference!

PARENT
WORKSHOPS:

Attend workshops that focus on student development, technology, academic support, and mental health. Stay tuned for upcoming events in the Events Calendar.

ACADEMIC SUPPORT FOR PARENTS

Understanding Graduation Requirements

It’s important for parents to understand the graduation criteria so they can support their child's academic journey:

  • Graduation Requirements:
    • Review the Florida Department of Education Graduation Requirements to understand the courses, credits, and assessments needed for graduation.
  • iTech Graduation Pathways
    • Explore the iTech Graduation Pathways for each program. Whether your child is on the ERP, GIS, or iCode track, we’ve outlined specific milestones and requirements for each pathway.
College and Career Readiness
  • College Application & Admissions Timeline:
    • Check out the College Application Timeline to stay informed about important deadlines, required documents, and application steps for your child’s college journey
  • Scholarship Opportunities:
    • We regularly update a list of scholarships available to iTech students. Parents can also access resources about scholarship applications through the Scholarship Portal.
  • Internships & Job Shadowing:
    • Encourage your child to take advantage of internships and job shadowing opportunities that will help them build their resumes and gain real-world experience. Visit the Internship Portal for opportunities.

RESOURCES FOR PARENTS

Mental Health and Wellness

Supporting the emotional and mental well-being of students is just as important as academic success. Here are resources for parents to monitor and support their child’s health:

Mental Health Support:

Learn more about the resources we provide for students who need mental health support. Visit our Mental Health Resource Page to explore counseling options, stress management tips, and crisis intervention.

Student Counseling Services:

Meet our school counselor, Ms. Tyra Tate, who is available to help your child navigate academic pressures and personal challenges. You can contact her directly via email or call 305-762-5000.

Parent Resources for Social-Emotional Learning:

Find tips and tools for helping your child develop emotional intelligence, conflict resolution skills, and coping mechanisms.

Parent-Teacher Communication

Staying Informed

We strive to keep you in the loop with regular updates on your child's progress, school activities, and events.

Parent Portal Access:

Access the Parent Portal to view your child's grades, attendance, and course information. Stay informed and communicate directly with teachers if necessary.

School Newsletters:

Sign up for the iTech Newsletters to receive monthly updates on school events, student achievements, and academic information.

Parent-Teacher Conferences:

We encourage regular communication with teachers. Join us for parent-teacher conferences, where you can discuss your child's progress and set goals together.

SCHOOL EVENTS AND IMPORTANT DATES

Upcoming Events for Parents and Families

Stay informed about events at iTech that are specifically geared toward parents and families:

  • Back-to-School Night:
    • Join us for the annual Back-to-School Night to meet your child’s teachers, learn about the curriculum, and connect with other parents.
  • Annual School Fundraisers:
    • We host several fundraising events throughout the year. Your participation helps enhance our school programs, extracurriculars, and resources.
  • Open Houses and Information Sessions:
    • Attend our Open Houses and information sessions to learn more about what iTech offers, including updates on curriculum, extracurriculars, and college prep.
  • Parent Workshops:
    • Participate in workshops that offer practical advice and strategies for helping your child succeed academically and socially. Check the Events Calendar for details.

PARENT-TEACHER COMMUNICATION

Parents play a crucial role in the college preparation process. Here's how you can support your child:

College Application Assistance:

iTech provides resources and workshops to help your child with the college application process. Parents can attend these sessions to learn how to guide their children through application deadlines, essays, and interviews.

FAFSA and Financial Aid:

Learn about the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and other financial aid resources. We provide workshops for parents and students to complete the FAFSA and explore scholarship opportunities.
