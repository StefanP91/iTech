WELCOME TO THE PARENT’S CORNER

This section is designed to keep you informed and engaged in your child's educational journey at iTech @ Thomas A. Edison Educational Center. Here, you'll find helpful resources, important school information, and opportunities to support your child's academic and extracurricular success.

IMPORTANT SCHOOL INFORMATION

School Policies and Guidelines

Student Code
of Conduct

Familiarize yourself with the Student Code of Conduct to understand the behavior and discipline expectations for students.

Parent-Student
Handbook:

Access the comprehensive Parent-Student Handbook for details about school rules, policies, safety guidelines, and school procedures. This document helps you understand what’s expected from students and parents.

Uniform and
Dress Code:

Learn about the required Uniform and Dress Code for iTech students. Ensure that your child is prepared for a professional learning environment.

Anti-Discrimination
Policies:

iTech is committed to creating an inclusive and respectful environment for all. Review our Anti-Discrimination Policy in English, Spanish, or Creole.

PARENT INVOLVEMENT

We encourage parents to be active participants in their child's education.
There are several ways you can engage with iTech:

PTSA (PARENT TEACHER
STUDENT ASSOCIATION):

Join our PTSA to collaborate with other parents, teachers, and staff in organizing events, fundraising, and creating a positive school community.

VOLUNTEER
OPPORTUNITIES:

Parents are encouraged to volunteer for events, help with school activities, or provide mentorship opportunities for students. Your involvement makes a big difference!

PARENT
WORKSHOPS:

Attend workshops that focus on student development, technology, academic support, and mental health. Stay tuned for upcoming events in the Events Calendar.
